Vantage is a toolkit for photo- and video-based spatial reconstruction designed for investigative journalists and fact-checkers by krisenstab.

We provide open source tools and guides for spatial journalism. Our web apps are free to use, run entirely in your browser and keep your data on your device.

Initial development was funded by the German Federal Ministry of Education and Research and the Prototype Fund.
