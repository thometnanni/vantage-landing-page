Funded by Prototype Fund, Vantage reconstructs the spatial context of video recordings.

Vantage is a tool designed for fact-checkers, investigative journalists, and Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) practitioners – particularly those working independently or in smaller teams.

It combines photomatching with support for geospatial data, videos and images, allowing researchers to verify content, reconstruct events, and gain new insights—all within a, web-based platform.

The platform projects these elements onto 3D environments, enabling accurate scene reconstructions, spatial analysis, and the ability to compare multiple viewpoints in a unified environment.

Vantage is open-source and freely accessible, ensuring that investigative tools are within reach for everyone, regardless of resources.
