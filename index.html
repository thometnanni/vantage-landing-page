Funded by Prototype Fund, Vantage reconstructs the spacial context of video recordings.

It's a tool designed for OSINT practitioners, fact-checkers, and investigative journalists, particularly those working independently or in smaller teams.

Vantage makes it easier to analyze and understand the wider spatial setting of videos.

Using photomatching techniques, Vantage aims to make these complex methodologies accessible to smaller research teams, enabling them to perform in-depth investigations with ease.

The platform is a gateway to in-depth exploration, echoing forensic methodologies, yet freely accessible and open-source.
