Funded by Prototype Fund, Vantage reconstructs the spacial context of video recordings.

Vantage is a tool designed for fact-checkers, investigative journalists and OSINT practitioners, particularly those working independently or in smaller teams.

It leverages photomatching techniques to handle 3D material, geospatial data, videos, and images directly within a web-based platform.

By projecting these elements onto dynamic 3D environments, Vantage allows researchers to reconstruct scenes and analyze spatial relationships within footage.

The platform is a gateway to in-depth exploration, echoing forensic methodologies, yet freely accessible and open-source.
